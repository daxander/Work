Mufftastic

Healthy Quinoa Muffins

Gluten Free Muffins with endless possible combinations!

You Will Need

Dry Ingredients:

  • 1 1/4 Cup Quinoa Flakes
  • 1/2 Cup Rice Flour (Both White and Brown work great!)
  • 1 Tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 Tsp Baking Soda

Wet Ingredients:

  • 1/4 Cup Plain Yoghurt
  • 3 Medium Sized Ripe Bananas
  • 1/2 Cup Honey
  • 1/3 Cup Grapeseed Oil
  • 2 Eggs

Last Pieces of The Puzzle:

  • 1/4 Cup Your Choice of Chopped Nuts
  • 1/4 to 1/2 Cup Your Choice Chopped Fruit

Suggested Combinations:

- Peach & Walnut - Blueberry & Pecan - Pear & Almond - Dark Chocolate, Raspberry & Hazelnut -

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Mix all Dry Ingredients in mixing bowl and set to the side
  3. Blend all Wet Ingredients in a blender and blend until mixture is fully mixed
  4. Fold the Wet mixture into the Dry Mixture (No need to over mix)
  5. Stir in chopped fruit and nuts
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until done
  7. Enjoy your tasty muffins!!!! (Maybe let them cool down a bit!)
